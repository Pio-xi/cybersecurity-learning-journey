# Ethics and Legal Rules

Cybersecurity must be practiced ethically and legally.

## My Rules

I will only test:

- My own systems
- Local lab environments
- Intentionally vulnerable applications
- Training platforms
- Systems where I have written permission

I will not:

- Scan real companies without permission
- Attempt to bypass login systems without authorization
- Access private data
- Exploit public websites
- Share harmful instructions
- Use cybersecurity knowledge to harm others

## Why Permission Matters

Even if the intention is educational, testing a system without permission can be illegal and harmful.

Professional cybersecurity work requires clear scope, written authorization, and responsible reporting.

## Key Lesson

Ethical hacking is only ethical when it is authorized.
