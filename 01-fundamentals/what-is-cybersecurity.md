# What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, applications, devices, and data from unauthorized access, misuse, damage, disruption, or theft.

## Why Cybersecurity Matters

Modern organizations depend on digital systems for communication, payments, records, customer data, school data, business operations, and decision-making.

If these systems are not protected, attackers may steal data, disrupt operations, damage reputation, or cause financial loss.

## Main Areas of Cybersecurity

- Network security
- Application security
- Cloud security
- Identity and access management
- Data protection
- Incident response
- Security awareness
- Governance, risk, and compliance
