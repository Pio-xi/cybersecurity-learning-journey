# CIA Triad

## Meaning

The CIA triad stands for:

- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Availability

## Confidentiality

Confidentiality means protecting information from unauthorized access.

Example:
A school system should only allow authorized staff to view student records.

## Integrity

Integrity means protecting information from unauthorized modification.

Example:
A student report card should not be changed by someone who does not have permission.

## Availability

Availability means systems and data should be accessible when needed.

Example:
A school fees system should be available when the bursar needs to process payments.

## Zimbabwean Business Example

A local clinic, school, or small business needs confidentiality, integrity, and availability to protect customer, student, or patient data.

## Key Lesson

Cybersecurity is about protecting data and systems from being accessed, changed, or disrupted by unauthorized people.
